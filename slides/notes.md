When do NDVI and EVI show normal water levels but NDWI shows water stress?
* NDVI and EVI primarily respond to canopy chlorophyll and leaf area index
    * they integrate over visible and NIR reflectance regime that tracks greenness and canopy density
* NDWI OTOH is more directly sensitive to leaf water content in the "spongy mesophyll"
    * spongy mesophyll - loose group of unorganized cells located between palisade mesophyll and abaxial epidermis
    * note: there is considerable variation in ratio of the mesophyll layers between species,
        indicating another parameter to measure and track and reason about
